Experiencing Any of These Problems?... Compass is the Solution

Sales Staff Giving Away Your Margin

Ever had a salesperson throw in an accessory for free in order to get a sale but expect full commission for selling the equipment at book price? Compass solves this by creating a parts list for each job based on what was sold.

Missing Documents and Contracts

Ever had a customer call into the office and discuss a contractual matter and you can't locate the paperwork? Compass takes care of this by storing an electronic copy of every sales presentation and contract created by the salesperson.

Installing Equipment That Doesn't Fit

Ever had your installer call back into the office and tell you that the new equipment doesn't fit? Compass takes care of this by providing the salesperson with the exact height, width, and depth measurements on any equipment solutions that they are proposing for the customer.

Overbooking Installation Technicians

Ever have a salesperson promise a customer that you can install the new equipment tomorrow without first checking with the installation manager? Compass takes care of this by requiring the salesperson to record the installation confirmation number in the system. Without the confirmation number, the installation date cannot be promised to the customer.

Sending Parts Runners to Job Sites

Ever have to send another employee to the installation site with a part that was needed to complete the job? Compass takes care of this by providing an exhaustive checklist of information that the salesperson will fill out to ensure that all necessary parts are ordered and taken to the job site by the installers.

Not Knowing the Status of your Expensive Sales Leads

Ever have a salesperson leave your company and wonder what the status is for all those unsold sales leads? Compass solves this by keeping track of all the information for every sales lead within your company. The Sales Manager portal allows for real-time access to all sales lead details.
